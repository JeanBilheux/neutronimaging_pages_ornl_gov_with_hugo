  • Short Bio ...
    Dr. Hassina Bilheux obtained her Ph.D. in Physics at the Univ. of Versailles, France.
    Her work focused on plasma physics at the Oak Ridge National Laboratory’s Physics Division.
    She has developed neutron imaging capabilities at ORNL’s High Flux Isotope Reactor CG-1D beamline and is prototyping neutron imaging at the Spallation Neutron Source. Her interests comprise the development of advanced neutron imaging techniques at the Spallation Neutron Source for material and biological applications.
